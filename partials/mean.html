MUNCH

Restaurant Management App
Colleagues: Miles Trotter Chris Jaglois

Used: Javascript, MongoDB + Mongoose, Express.js + Socket.io, Angular.js, Node.js, Responsive Web Design

MUNCH unifies all aspects of restaurant management.
Edit the menu, message workers, check tips, change pay, pick up shifts, and more.

Personal contributions:

Login/Registration & Dashboard pages (extensive jQuery)

Dashboard messages, specials, & scribbles (Angular.js & Socket.io)

Profile editing (Angular.js)

Personnel editing (Angular.js)

Server-side controller and database management, & account type management (MongoDB + Mongoose)
